Bringing 'the crêpe bretonne' to Paris:

Crêperie parisienne, is born in Brittany, France , with two friends the idea of bringing to Paris the traditional Brittany culture to make it feel like home. The restaurant is summarized in few words as heaven, with the main character being the famous Crêpes.

What are Crêpes? A French sort of pancake, made of a thin batter containing flour, eggs, melted butter, salt, milk and water. Crêpes can be filled with a variety of sweet or savory mictures. handmade with love, and filled with the best quality ingredients.

That is why we established Crêperie parisienne in Paris, not only as a dream, but to establish a place in Paris to share a piece of the the french culture
